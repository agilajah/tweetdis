Welcome to tweetDIS GitHub Page.

A smart web application that convert RAW data from twitter to information which is useful, curated, and organized in ways that allows you to focus in categories that matter to you in deep and thoughful ways. tweetDIS divides information to five categories related to government regional offices in Bandung and create clear categorization among them. So you can know.better. Visit our website to try it out.
